Clairo

Clairo is a really cool indie pop artist that I discovered about a year ago with her song "Bags." Her recent album Charm is one of my favorites of all time, and I would love to see her in concert sometime!

  • My favorite song by her is "Amoeba"
  • I named my car after her song "Juna"
  • "Juna" is also my #3 most played song of the last six months!

boygenius

boygenius is an indie pop supergroup comprised of three amazing artists - Phoebe Bridgers, Lucy Dacus, and Julien Baker. I love their solo music outside of the band, but they have a lovely vibe together that makes boygenius different. Please come back from hiatus!

  • My favorite song by them is "$20."
  • Lucy Dacus played a show in downtown Harrisonburg at Clementine back in 2015!
  • "Not Strong Enough" is my #7 most played song of the last six months!

  • Website
  • Spotify

Ben Folds

Ben Folds is a quirky piano rock star, who was most popular in the 1990s as the frontman of Ben Folds Five. His music varies between energetically unhinged and calmly chilled out, which is a fun dynamic. His collaborators vary from Regina Spektor ..to William Shatner. Name one thing in common between them.

  • My favorite song by him is "Emaline."
  • I saw him in concert in July 2023.
  • The live version of "Emaline" is my #2 most played song of the last four weeks!
  • Wikipedia
  • Website
  • Spotify

beabadoobee

beabadoobee is an indie pop singer who started out making bedroom pop, but gained popularity after she opened for Taylor Swift at the Eras Tour. She has a fun vibe and writes sincerely in all of her songs.

  • My favorite song by her is "Apple Cider."
  • She collaborated with Clairo on a new version of her song "Glue Song."
  • "The Man Who Left Too Soon" is my #45 most played song of the last six months!
  • Wikipedia
  • Website
  • Spotify

M83

M83 is an electronic music act who have been around the mainstream for a while, but have never truly been "popular." Led by Anthony Gonzalez, they've had some hit songs and made soundtracks for some movies, but they still have a somewhat indie edge.

  • My favorite song by them is "Ok Pal."
  • Their song "Wait" was prominently featured in the movie "The Fault in Our Stars."
  • "My Tears Are Becoming A Sea" is my #17 most played song of the last four weeks!
